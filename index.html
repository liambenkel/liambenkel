Teachable Machine - Cats, Dogs, and Birds Welcome to my interactive machine learning demo

This model serves as an educational tool for a younger audience. While some cats, dogs, and birds may have different characteristics, they all fall under this one of the three categories! For example, a Toucan does not look like an Owl, but they are both considered birds, some dogs, like Australian kelpies, resemble a cat but are not a cat. It's important to be able to distinguish between the three animals to create a general knowledge of these categories.
